About Gerfalcon:

Gerfalcon is a historic 34-foot motor yacht, originally built in 1937 and renowned for her service in the Dunkirk evacuation during World War II. As a vessel of significant historical importance, Gerfalcon has participated in numerous commemorative events, including the Queen's Diamond Jubilee Pageant in 2012. Currently, she requires extensive restoration to prevent further deterioration and to restore her to seaworthiness.
